FEATURE EXTRACTION

Term Frequency - (TF) :
Term Frequency is defined as the number of times a word can occur inside the document.

Inverse Document Frequency - (IDF) :
Inverse document frequency for a word can be defined as the reciprocal of number of documents in which the word appears.

Term frequency - Inverse Document Frequency - (TF-IDF) :
TF-IDF for a given word can be defined as the product of term frequency and inverse document frequency.
